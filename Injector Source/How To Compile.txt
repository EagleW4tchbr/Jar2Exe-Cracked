To Build With Resources (icon+info) Use:

To Change .exe Info Edit resources.rc

Then Use

"PATH\TO\mingw32\bin\windres.exe" resources.rc -O coff -o resources.res

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

And Then Compile cpp To exe With Resources Info

"PATH\TO\mingw32\bin\g++.exe" Jar2exe_Injector.cpp resources.res -o Jar2exe_Injector.exe -std=c++17 -municode -mwindows -static -s
